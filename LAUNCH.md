## 100-word pitch

SIFARISH is a job-hunt chief of staff with one rule: *Compile truth. Draft everything. Send nothing.*
In a market where 91% of recruiters hunt fabricated resumes and pretty resumes die in ATS parsers, it
keeps a two-tier evidence ledger of what you can *provably* do, hunts AI-first roles from keyless
public job feeds, and **compiles** — never writes — an ATS-plain resume, cover letter, and outreach
draft per company. A bullet without evidence is a compile error. The generated PDF is parse-back-tested
so the ATS reads exactly what you wrote. It runs with zero API keys. It never presses Send — you do.
